🧠 General Trivia & Did-You-Know

  • The national anthems of Spain and Bosnia & Herzegovina have no official lyrics — they're purely instrumental.
  • Switzerland legally treats some social animals as needing company: owning just one guinea pig can be against the law.
  • The combined weight of all the ants on Earth is roughly in the same ballpark as the combined weight of all humans.
  • Tennis balls were white until 1972, when "optic yellow" was adopted because it showed up better on color TV.
  • Some freshwater turtles can absorb oxygen through their cloaca — effectively breathing through their rear — while hibernating underwater.
  • The largest living organism on Earth is a honey fungus in Oregon's Blue Mountains, sprawling across about 3.7 square miles.
  • The medical name for "brain freeze" is sphenopalatine ganglioneuralgia.
  • Your "funny bone" isn't a bone at all — the zing comes from bumping the ulnar nerve at your elbow.
  • The dollar sign ($) most likely evolved from the abbreviation for the Spanish peso.
  • "Strengths" is one of the longest English words built around a single vowel.
  • Recycling a single aluminum can saves enough energy to run a TV for about three hours.
  • There are more atoms in a single glass of water than there are glasses of water in all the world's oceans.
