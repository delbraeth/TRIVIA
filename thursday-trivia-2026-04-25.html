🧩 General Trivia

🍌 Bananas are berries — but strawberries, raspberries and blackberries technically are not.
🗼 The Eiffel Tower can be ~6 inches taller in summer — iron expands in heat.
🐦‍⬛ A group of crows is called a "murder."
⏳ Cleopatra lived closer in time to the Moon landing than to the building of the Great Pyramid of Giza.
🪐 Saturn would float if you could find a bathtub big enough — its average density is less than water's.
🟢 Oranges weren't named after the color — the fruit existed first; the color was named after it. Early oranges were green.
🧊 Antarctica is the world's largest desert — defined by precipitation, not heat.
🟫 Wombat poop is cube-shaped — sculpted inside their intestines so it doesn't roll away (they use it to mark territory).
🗽 The Statue of Liberty's 7 crown spikes represent the seven continents and seven seas.
📚 "Set" has more definitions than any other word in English — over 430 in the OED.
🦷 Sharks predate trees — sharks have been around ~450 million years; trees evolved ~390 million years ago.
🦅 The bald eagle isn't bald — "bald" is from an old English word meaning "white-headed."
