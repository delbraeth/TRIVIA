🏭 Founded & Invented

  • Dr Pepper — invented year/inventor?
    1885, Charles Alderton at Morrison's Old Corner Drug Store, Waco TX (oldest major US soft drink)
  • Hoover vacuum — who patented the original electric upright (1908)?
    James Murray Spangler (sold patent to W.H. Hoover; Hoover Suction Sweeper Co. 1908)
  • Bubble gum (Dubble Bubble) — inventor/year?
    Walter Diemer at Fleer Chewing Gum Co., 1928 (Philadelphia; accountant, not a chemist)
  • Cheerios — original name and year?
    "CheeriOats," 1941 (General Mills); renamed Cheerios in 1945
