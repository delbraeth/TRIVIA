🧠 General Trivia

A single lightning bolt packs enough energy to toast roughly 100,000 slices of bread.
A group of jellyfish is called a "smack."
Africa is the only continent that lies in all four hemispheres.
Pineapple's enzyme bromelain breaks down protein — so the fruit "bites back" as you eat it.
One day on Mercury (sunrise to sunrise) lasts about 176 Earth days — longer than its 88-day year.
Your body contains roughly as many bacterial cells as human cells.
The Hundred Years' War actually lasted 116 years (1337–1453).
The world's first webcam (Cambridge, 1991) was aimed at a coffee pot so workers could see if it was full.
Sea otters have the densest fur of any animal — up to ~1 million hairs per square inch.
Istanbul is the only major city that straddles two continents (Europe and Asia).
The word "alphabet" comes from the first two Greek letters: alpha + beta.
