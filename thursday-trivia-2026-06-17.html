🧠 General Trivia & Did-You-Know

  • Honey never spoils — archaeologists have found 3,000-year-old honey in Egyptian tombs that was still perfectly edible.
  • Bananas are botanically berries, but strawberries aren't berries at all.
  • Octopuses have three hearts and blue, copper-based blood.
  • Sharks are older than trees: sharks have swum the oceans for about 450 million years, trees appeared roughly 390 million years ago.
  • A single bolt of lightning is about five times hotter than the surface of the Sun.
  • The shortest war in history — the Anglo-Zanzibar War of 1896 — lasted roughly 38 minutes.
  • Venus spins in the opposite direction to almost every other planet, so on Venus the Sun rises in the west.
  • A group of flamingos is called a "flamboyance."
  • Scotland's official national animal is the unicorn.
  • Fredric Baur, who designed the Pringles can, was so proud of it that he had part of his ashes buried in one.
  • Under the right conditions, hot water can freeze faster than cold water — a still-debated puzzle called the Mpemba effect.
  • There are more possible chess games than there are atoms in the observable universe.
