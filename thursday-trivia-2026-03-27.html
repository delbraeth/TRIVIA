🧩 General Trivia

  • 🍔 Food & Drink: Honey never spoils. Archaeologists have found 3,000-year-old honey in Egyptian tombs that was still perfectly edible.
  • 🎵 Pop Culture: The Wilhelm Scream — a stock sound effect first recorded in 1951 — has appeared in over 400 films and TV shows, from Star Wars to Lord of the Rings.
  • 🏅 Sports: A regulation NBA basketball must bounce to a height of 49–54 inches when dropped from 6 feet onto a hardwood floor.
