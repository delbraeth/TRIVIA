🧠 General Trivia

🔬 Science: What gas makes up about 78% of Earth's atmosphere?
Nitrogen
Oxygen is only ~21%. Most people guess oxygen!
🌍 Geography: What is the capital of Australia?
Canberra
Not Sydney or Melbourne — Canberra was purpose-built as a compromise capital in 1913.
🌍 Geography: What is the capital of Canada?
Ottawa
Not Toronto or Montreal. Ottawa has been the capital since 1857.
🌍 State Capital: What is the capital of Montana?
Helena
Not Billings (the largest city). Helena became the state capital in 1889.
🌍 State Capital: What is the capital of Vermont?
Montpelier
With fewer than 8,000 people, it's the smallest U.S. state capital by population.
🍕 Food: What Italian city is pizza Margherita named after? (And why?)
Naples — named for Queen Margherita of Savoy
In 1889, chef Raffaele Esposito made it to honor the queen — red (tomato), white (mozzarella), green (basil) = Italian flag.
🍺 Food & Drink: What country produces the most wine in the world?
Italy
Italy edges France and Spain most years, followed by the USA and Australia.
🎬 Pop Culture: What 1994 film features the line "Life is like a box of chocolates"?
Forrest Gump
Tom Hanks won the Oscar for Best Actor for the role. The correct quote is actually "was like."
🦅 Wildcard: How many feathers does a bald eagle have?
About 7,000
Despite their imposing wingspan (up to 8 feet), their feathers weigh less than their skeleton.
🏆 Sports: Who holds the MLB record for most career stolen bases?
Rickey Henderson — 1,406
He broke Lou Brock's record in 1991 and was famously his own biggest fan. "Rickey is the best!"
📜 History: Which U.S. president was the first to live in the White House?
John Adams (1800)
George Washington oversaw its construction but never lived there. Adams moved in while it was still under construction.
