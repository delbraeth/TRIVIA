🧠 General Trivia & Did-You-Know

  • Cows have regional "accents" in their moos.
  • On average, vending machines cause more deaths per year than sharks do.
  • Twitter's old bird logo had a name — "Larry," after NBA legend Larry Bird.
  • Crocodiles cannot stick out their tongues.
  • Pound cake is named for its original recipe: a pound each of butter, sugar, flour, and eggs.
  • Point Nemo, the most remote spot in the ocean, is so far from land that the nearest humans are often astronauts on the ISS passing overhead.
  • Because of Earth's equatorial bulge, the summit of Ecuador's Mount Chimborazo — not Everest — is the point farthest from the center of the Earth.
  • Tungsten has the highest melting point of any metal, about 6,192°F (3,422°C).
  • Axolotls can regrow lost limbs, parts of their heart, and even portions of their brain.
  • The fear of Friday the 13th has a name: paraskevidekatriaphobia.
